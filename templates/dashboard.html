Attendance Distribution

Matplotlib + Seaborn
Histogram + KDE overlay showing how attendance patterns differ across performance grades. Excellent students cluster above 85%; Needs Improvement students show a long left tail.

Marks Distribution

Matplotlib + Seaborn
Violin plot (left) shows full distribution shape and quartiles per grade. Box plot (right) highlights median, IQR, and outliers — Excellent students have tighter, higher ranges.

Feature Correlation Heatmap

Seaborn · Pearson r
Pearson correlation matrix of all 6 input features (lower triangle). Study hours ↔ Attendance shows the strongest positive correlation (r ≈ 0.45), confirming that disciplined students maintain both habits simultaneously.

Performance Category Breakdown

Matplotlib
Donut chart (left) shows class proportions — dominated by Excellent due to the dataset's positive skew. Horizontal bar (right) gives exact counts. SMOTE was applied during training to balance these classes for unbiased learning.

Study Hours vs Previous Marks

Matplotlib · Scatter
Each point is one student. The gradient from red (Needs Improvement) to green (Excellent) shows a clear diagonal trend — more study hours + higher prior marks strongly predict better outcomes.
